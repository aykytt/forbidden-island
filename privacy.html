Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026

Forbidden Island Companion ("the app") is a sound and atmosphere assistant for the Forbidden Island board game. This policy explains how we handle data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

Philips Hue Integration

When using the Hue + Sound mode, the app communicates exclusively with your Philips Hue Bridge on your local network. No data is transmitted to external servers. Your network credentials and bridge information never leave your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date.
